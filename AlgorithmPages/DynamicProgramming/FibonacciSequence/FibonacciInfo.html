Fibonacci Sequence

A sequence where the next term is the sum of the previous two terms.

Example of a Fibonacci Sequence: 0, 1, 1, 2, 3, 5, 8, 13, 21


The sequence starts off with two numbers, 0 and 1. When those are summed up, it equals 1. The second 1 in the sequence then gets added to the previous 1 to equal 2. 2 then adds to the previous 1 to equal 2. The sequence continues to build by summing up the latest number to the previous number.
